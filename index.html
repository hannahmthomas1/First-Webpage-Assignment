Renaming Trees

Better Names

Have you ever been on a nice walk or hike in the mountains when a well-meaning friend or family member interupts the peaceful silence by deciding to quiz you about local foliage? Well I have. I love plants, but I am not going to remember their names. But don't fear! There is a simple solution! We can make this world a better place by giving plants better names.

The Big Idea

This problem does have a solution! That is, to have trees named by people who don't know anything about trees. That (likely) means you! When you lose something, the best place to put it next time is the first place you looked. So, better names for trees are the first words we think of when we see it!

For Example:

Pine Tree
Dark Pointy Tree
Oak
Spotted white trunk with round leaves
Redwood
Really Big

Why try?

According to globaltrees.org, there are 60,065 tree species. The average adult knows 20,000-35,000 words. Even if we tried, we would be unable to remember them all. Is it really worth trying?

How to create a name:

  1. Look at tree
  2. Write down the first descriptive words that come to your mind
  3. Share your new name creation
    • Submit the name with the link at the top of the page
    • Share on Social Media

Use #RenameTrees when sharing on social media.
